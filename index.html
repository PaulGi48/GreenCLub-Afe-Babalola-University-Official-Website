About Us

We are a group of students at Afe Babalola University committed to promoting environmental awareness and sustainability.

SUSTAINABILITY

Working with you to get under the skin of your current operation, we will deliver a comprehensive audit and survey of your energy usage. What fuels do you use right now? Where are they sourced from? What do they cost and is there a better, greener and more efficient solution?

INNOVATION

Technology and renewable energy are good friends! We can help your business adapt and grow through the use of smart digital management systems, accounting for current development plans and future capacity requirements.

GENERATION

Generate your own green renewable energy, and you could become an energy provider too with the ability to sell surplus energy to your own local market! Today this is a viable alternative to reliance on the grid, and can reduce overheads and dependency.

ACCREDITATION

Any business taking steps towards renewable energy solutions deserves credit. GreenClub works closely with the GEO Foundation to dovetail our programmes and services, and can offer you internationally endorsed GEO-certified accreditation.
